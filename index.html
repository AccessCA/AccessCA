September 6, 2016

LGBTQ Progress in the Media

Sarah Kate Ellis

President and CEO of GLAAD

LGBTQ representation in media is critical for the path towards social acceptance. Although strides toward equality have been substantial in the past few years, there are many more milestones to meet before the LGBTQ community can reach the ultimate goal of acceptance. In this talk, Sarah Kate Ellis will make the case for why LGBTQ media presence is critical and how we can work together to realize full acceptance for the LGBTQ community.

October 11, 2016

The American DREAMer: From Undocumented to Wall Street

Julissa Arce

Immigrant Rights Advocate and Former Wall Street Executive

"The American DREAMer: From Undocumented to Wall Street and Immigration Advocacy" Julissa Arce built a successful career on Wall Street working at Goldman Sachs and Merrill Lynch and had seemingly achieved the American Dream of wealth and status, and yet she was not part of what defined American. Julissa will share how she asked herself, what if she defined American.
