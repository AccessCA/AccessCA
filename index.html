What is Access California?

Access California is a platform that condenses the eligibility requirements for public programs into one simple form. Currently we help determine if you're eligible for:


CalFresh

Get money to help put food on the table.


MediCal

Get insurance to help you stay healthy.


CalWORKS

Get cash for housing, food and more.
