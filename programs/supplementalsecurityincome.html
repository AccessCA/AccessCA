Supplemental Security Income (SSI)

Get cash for housing, food and clothing

How does SSI work?

Supplemental Security Income (SSI) helps aged, blind, and disabled people that need housing, food and clothing. The amount you will receive depends on a number of factors. Click here to find more information.

If you receive SSI, you can usually get insurance through MediCal automatically. However, if you receive SSI, you will not be eligible for CalFresh (also known as SNAP/Food Stamps).

How do I apply for SSI?


  1. Fill out an application online, call to schedule an appointment, or visit your local Social Security Office

    You can also call 1-800-772-1213 (or TTY 1-800-325-0778 if you are deaf or hard of hearing) to schedule an appointment to apply.


  2. Wait to hear back from the Social Security Administration

  3. Start using your benefits
