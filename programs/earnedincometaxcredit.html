Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)

Get a refund or reduce your taxes

How does the EITC work?

The Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) is a tax refund for low- and moderate-income households, especially ones with children. EITC is also called EIC or Earned Income Credit.

To receive the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), you must file a tax return even if you don't owe taxes or aren't required to file taxes.

How do I apply for EITC?


  1. Gather important documents and claim the EITC on your tax return


  2. Wait for your refund

    If you claim the EITC, your entire refund will be delayed until mid-February. You should receive your tax refund sometime after mid-February.
