CalWORKS

Get cash for housing, food and more

How does CalWORKS work?

CalWORKS helps families with children. Low-income families that need housing, food, utilities, clothing or medical care can get immediate short-term help. Families that need more assistance can receive money each month to help pay for housing, food and other necessary expenses.

The amount your family will receive depends on a number of factors, including your family's income, the number of people who are eligible, and the special needs of any of those family members.

How do I apply for CalWORKS?


  1. Fill out an application online or mail one in


  2. Hear back from the county

    Your county should reach out to you within 45 days with a final decision about your benefits.


  3. Start using your benefits
